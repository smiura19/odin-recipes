Thai Curry

Thai curry is a simple dish that is completely customizble. It can be made with ingredients you have in your pantry and fridge.

Ingredients

  • Thai curry paste
  • Coconut milk
  • protein of choice ie chicken, tofu, etc.
  • vegetables of choice

Steps

  1. Sautee onions, garlic, and ginger
  2. Add and sautee protein and vegetables
  3. Add curry paste to pan and sautee for 5 min
  4. Add coconut milk and stir
  5. Bring to boil and simmer for 15 min
